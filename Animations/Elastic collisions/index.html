Colliding Blocks

This is a simulation of elastically colliding blocks inspired by this 3Blue1Brown video about the connection between the number of collisions and $\pi$. During collisions, both energy \[ E = \frac{1}{2}m_1 v_1^2 + \frac{1}{2}m_2 v_2^2 \] and momentum \[ P = m_1 v_1 + m_2 v_2 \] is conserved. This means that if we rescale the axes, then the points $\left(\sqrt{m_1}v_1,\sqrt{m_2}v_2\right)$ will lie on a circle of radius $\sqrt{2E}$.
